What is this travel blog about?

That probably is the most obvious question, isn’t it? We started this website
at the end of 2013 as a way to document our overland journey from south to north without flying.
A few years later and this is now one of the most popular adventure travel blogs on the internet,
and we have to admit we’re pretty proud of that!

We never thought this site would grow into what it is now, with the blog being our full-time jobs
and over 250’000 social media followers from around the world all checking in to see what’s
happening as we travel non-stop.
